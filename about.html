About Jenna

I'm a design generalist who crafts simple solutions to complex problems. I love getting input and collaborating with diverse teams to reach the best outcomes.

I began my design career back in 2014, learning all things User Experience in the early days of General Assembly. Before the program ended, I signed up to join the team at Brave UX. Working at an agency, I encountered a wide array of problems and developed a strong foundation in information architecture. I had the opportunity to work with a variety of clients, ranging from top names like AOL, Royal Caribbean, and Gensler to startups and local D.C. heroes.

After nearly 3 years of agency life, I dove into product design at a fintech startup called MotoRefi. Working as a designer embedded in the engineering team, I strengthened my skills in front-end coding, visual design, and user research (among others).

Currently I'm freelancing and keeping an eye out for my next great challenge. If you're a team looking for a versatile designer, shoot me an email, I'd love to hear from you.
