About Jenna

I'm a design generalist who crafts simple solutions to complex problems.

I began my design career back in 2014, graduating from an early cohort of General Assembly D.C. By the time the program ended, I had signed up to join the team at Brave UX. I worked on a wide array of design problems, collaborating with a variety of clients like AOL and Gensler.

After 3 years of agency life, I dove into product design at MotoRefi, a fintech startup focused on loan refinance. As part of a small team, I led UX/UI design, user research, CSS polishes, and more.

In July 2020, I embarked on my next adventure and joined the product team at Higher Logic.

When I'm not working, you'll find me cooking, crosswording, and dreaming of my future farm. If you'd like to get in touch, I'd love to hear from you.
