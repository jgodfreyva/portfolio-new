About Jenna

I'm a design generalist who crafts simple solutions to complex problems.

I began my career in 2014, honing my UX skills at General Assembly and landing my first design position at Brave UX. I worked on a wide array of design problems and led IA, collaborating with a variety of clients like AOL and Gensler.

After 3 years of agency life, I dove into product design at MotoRefi, a fintech startup (now called Caribou) focused on loan refinance. As part of a small team, I led UX/UI design, user research, and CSS polishes.

In 2020, I joined the product team creating online communities at Higher Logic. I enjoy working cross-functionally and guiding UX for features like AI tools, editors, widgets, and more across a suite of products.

In my spare time, you'll find me cooking, puzzling, and mastering the art of being handy. If you'd like to get in touch, I'd love to hear from you.
